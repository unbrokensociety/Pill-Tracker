# 🚀 PillTracker v1.48 — Release Notes

### 🌟 What's New in Version 1.48

* ☁️ **Enhanced Background Cloud Storage & Automatic Sync**: Completely automated background synchronization. Cloud sync is silent and automatic, saving changes locally when offline and syncing automatically upon reconnecting to the network.
* 🔒 **Data Deletion & 30-Day Grace Period (Закон України «Про захист персональних даних» № 2297-VI)**: Users can now request complete deletion of their account and medical logs. In compliance with Ukrainian legislation and GDPR, a 30-day grace period is initiated during which you can cancel deletion at any time in Settings.
* 🛡️ **Comprehensive Privacy Policy with Ukrainian Legislation**: Expanded Privacy Policy with explicit references to Ukrainian privacy laws (ст. 15 Закону України «Про захист персональних даних» № 2297-VI, Закон України «Основи законодавства України про охорону здоров'я» № 2801-XII) and language switcher (UA/EN).
* 📧 **Streamlined Email Authentication & Email Confirmation Modal**: Optimized authentication flow for email accounts with clear verification and registration guidance. Removed redundant Google OAuth dependency for offline/independent release stability.
